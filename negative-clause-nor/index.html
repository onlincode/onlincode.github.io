NEGATIVE CLAUSE + nor | neither

auxiliary, C2, CLAUSES, CONJUNCTIONS, coordinating, FOCUS, inversion, NEGATIVE

Here are two overlapping C2 grammar points in the English Grammar Profile.

Point 129 in CLAUSES/coordinated is defined as:

combine a negative clause with an inverted clause with ‘nor’, to give focus.

Point 25 in CONJUNCTIONS/coordinating is defined as:

‘Neither’ or ‘Nor’ + inverted auxiliary or ‘be’ + subject to add to a previous related negative clause, to focus on an additional negative factor.

We can start by searching in iWeb for:

nor _V _P
