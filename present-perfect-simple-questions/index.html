present perfect simple questions + increasing range of verbs

B2, PAST, perfect, PRESENT, QUESTIONS, range

In the English Grammar Profile, B2 point 51 in the category of PAST and present perfect simple is defined as:

question form with an increasing range of verbs.

For example:

How much sleep have they actually lost?

TED

Looking for variable-length questions on iWeb is always a hard task.  Here is only one set pattern that finds some.  Note that we also need to search for ‘range of verbs’.
