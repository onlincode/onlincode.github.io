superlative adjective + noun + to infinitive

ADJECTIVES, article, B2, infinitive, superlative

B2 point 66 in the category of ADJECTIVES on the English Grammar Profile is defined as:

a superlative with a NOUN AND TO-INFINITIVE.

For example:

The most important thing to remember is his general presence.

listen

 

Immunization, that‘s the cheapest way to save a child‘s life.
