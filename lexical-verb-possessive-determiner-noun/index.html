We raised her to speak her mind.

listen

The phrase “speak her mind” means to express her thoughts and feelings honestly and openly, even if they are not popular or may upset others. It is a phrase that is often used to describe people who are not afraid to speak their truth, even if it means going against the grain.

In the sentence above, the speaker is saying that they taught their child to be honest and open about her thoughts and feelings. They wanted their child to feel comfortable expressing herself, even if it meant disagreeing with them or others.

to sit and collect your thoughts.

listen

The phrase “collect your thoughts” means to calm down and organize your thoughts. It is often used when someone is feeling overwhelmed or stressed. In the sentence above, the speaker is suggesting that the place they are in is a peaceful and calming place where someone could go to clear their head and think things through.
