Past simple + time adjuncts

adjuncts, B2, PAST, simple, time

In the English Grammar Profile, B2 point 62 in category of PAST is defined as:

a range of time adjuncts with the past simple.

This is quite ambiguous as to what constitutes this in the EGP exactly.  The examples all include a past simple verb and a time word but often with lots of words between, which makes it hard to get through using the iWeb corpus.  The best we can get is:

1 SAID AT THE TIME 11702 (these don’t always appear to be adjuncts)
2 DECIDED IT WAS TIME 10953
3 TOOK ME A WHILE 9249
4 TOOK A LONG TIME 7999
