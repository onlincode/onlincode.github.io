Do not (imperative)

B2, CLAUSES, emphasis, formal, imperatives, NEGATIVE

In the English Grammar Profile, B2 Point 99 in the category of  CLAUSES & imperatives is defined as:

‘DO NOT’ for EMPHASIS in formal contexts

We do not believe ‘formality’ is essential to make this structure B2.

*We have expanded this point because the first person imperative can be negated simply by inserting ‘not’ after the pronoun:

Let me not fall into temptation.
