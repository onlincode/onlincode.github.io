temporal shift & time words

B2, backshift, REPORTED SPEECH

In the English Grammar Profile, B2 point 16 in the category of REPORTED SPEECH is defined as:

events in the past with backshifts for tenses and words relating to time

Automatic searches for this are impossible but we can look in corpora for combinations of told * previous|following * _nnt since these all exist in the EGP examples.

Here, an advanced PELIC text from a grammar class really showcases a great variety of reported speech:

Korean,Female,5,g

She told me that her plans for the following vacation were [a] trip to California. She explained that she had wanted to go there for a long time. Also she reported that the weather of California is usually wonderful. Additionally, she admitted that her biggest pet peeve was winter and snow. When I asked the reason, she reminded me that she was from the country that doesn’t have winter. Finally she pointed out that she used to ride a bike when she was younger, but she didn’t’ do it anymore. She commented that she became timid as she grew up.
